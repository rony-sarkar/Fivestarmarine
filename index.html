We use a Viton wiper seal and a High Grade Urethane Oil Seal for this actuator. These materials are very expensive and will hold up for 20-30 years.

Extremely Important information in this next paragraph Only use Basic ATF (Automatic Transmission Fluid) NOT Dextron 4 or any modern transmission fluid or power steering fluid. Basic ATF can be purchased at any auto parts store its cheap and is the best thing for the application. Modern oils are mostly synthetic and full of swelling agents and high detergent properties this can degrade seals very quickly and swell new seals to the point of leaking. Also keep in mind synthetic oils should not be used in hydraulic systems as the molecules of the synthetic oils are so small they will slip past seals and leak. You can purchase ATF DEX/MERC 2 and or 3 for about $20 a gallon. Also please make sure your system is clean and free from debris and particles. Please also make sure your belt driven power steering pump is not burnt up from running without or low on fluid.
